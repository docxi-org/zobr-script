# Task 05: AI Safety Regulation — Analysis + ZS Export

## Your task

Analyze the current global debate on AI safety regulation.

Key questions:
- Who are the key actors (governments, organizations, companies)?
- What regulatory frameworks are being proposed or enacted?
- What tensions exist between innovation and safety?
- Where is the debate heading?

## Instructions

1. **Search the web** for current information on AI safety regulation (2025-2026 developments)
2. **Analyze the topic deeply** — identify stakeholders, positions, tensions, hidden interests
3. **Save your analysis** as `result.md` to the designated results directory
4. **Then create a reusable ZS script** (`reflection.zobr`) that captures the *reasoning pattern* you used — not the specific content, but the generalizable cognitive structure that could be applied to analyze *any* policy debate on emerging technology regulation
5. **Validate** the script by running: `node dist/cli.js <path-to-reflection.zobr>`
6. If validation reports errors — fix the script and re-validate until clean
7. Save the final validation output to `validation.txt`
